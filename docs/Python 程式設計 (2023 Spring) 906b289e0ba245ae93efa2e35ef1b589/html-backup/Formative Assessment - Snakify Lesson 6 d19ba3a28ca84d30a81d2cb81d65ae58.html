Formative Assessment - Snakify Lesson 6

This assessment is designed to test your understanding of Snakify Lesson 6: While loops. Snakify is an online platform that teaches Python programming through interactive exercises. Lesson 6 covers the basics of while loops, such as how to use them for repeated actions, how to control their termination condition, and how to avoid infinite loops. The assessment contains two parts: easy and slightly harder. Each part has 10 multiple-choice questions that cover different aspects of while loops, such as syntax, logic, and common applications. You will have 20 minutes to complete each part. The assessment will help you consolidate your knowledge of while loops and prepare you for more advanced topics in Python programming.
